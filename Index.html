Features

List of all the exclusive features of YouTube ReVanced App.

#1

Main Features:

  • Ad-blocking: YouTube ReVanced blocks all ads on the app, including banner ads, video ads, and sponsored content.
  • Background playback: With YouTube ReVanced, you can continue playing audio from a video even after you close the app or lock your screen.
  • Video downloading: You can download videos to your device for offline viewing, so you can watch them without an internet connection.
  • Picture-in-picture mode: This feature allows you to continue watching a video in a small window while you use other apps on your device.
  • Customization options: YouTube ReVanced offers a range of customization options, including changing the theme, color scheme, and other visual elements of the app.
  • Override video resolution: With this feature, you can force YouTube ReVanced to play videos at a specific resolution, even if the video's creator has set a lower quality.
  • Swipe controls: You can use swipe gestures to control the brightness and volume of the video you're watching.
  • Auto-replay: This feature automatically replays a video once it's finished, without the need to click the replay button.
  • Disable suggestions: You can disable video suggestions on the home screen and in the recommended videos section, so you can watch the videos you want without being distracted by other content.
  • No need for Google Play Services: YouTube ReVanced works on devices that do not have Google Play Services installed, making it a popular choice for users who prefer not to use Google's services or have rooted their Android devices.
#2

Customization:

    YouTube ReVanced offers a range of customization options that allow users to personalize the app to their liking. Some of the customization options available include:
  • Theme: You can choose between a dark or light theme for the app. This can be helpful for reducing eye strain, especially when using the app at night.
  • Colors: You can customize the colors of the app, including the background, text, and icons. This can help make the app look more unique and personalized.
  • Layout: You can change the layout of the app to suit your preferences. This includes options to change the size and position of the video player, as well as the arrangement of other elements in the app.
  • Language: You can choose from a variety of languages to use in the app, allowing you to use it in your preferred language.
  • Accessibility: YouTube ReVanced includes accessibility features such as text size adjustment and color inversion, making it easier to use for people with visual impairments.
  • Advanced options: For more advanced users, YouTube ReVanced also includes additional options such as the ability to force HDR mode or change the codec used for video playback.
#3

Sponsor Block:

  • Sponsor Block is a unique feature of YouTube ReVanced that allows users to skip past sponsored content in videos. When activated, Sponsor Block uses community-driven data to detect and skip past sections of a video that contain sponsorships or advertisements.
  • This feature works by analyzing user-generated timestamps of sponsored segments in videos, which are then compiled into a shared database. When a user watches a video that contains a sponsored segment, YouTube ReVanced checks the database to see if there is a corresponding timestamp for that segment. If there is, it skips the sponsored segment automatically, allowing the user to continue watching the video without interruptions.
  • Sponsor Block is a popular feature among YouTube ReVanced users, as it allows them to skip over unwanted sponsorships or advertisements in videos, resulting in a smoother and more enjoyable viewing experience. It also helps support content creators who may rely on sponsorships to generate income, as it only skips past sponsorships and not other content in the video.
#4

Return YouTube™ Dislike

  • Show YouTube Dislike count is a feature of YouTube ReVanced that allows users to view the dislike count on videos, even if the count has been hidden by the video uploader. This feature is useful for users who want to see the complete picture of a video's popularity, including both likes and dislikes.
  • The feature works by sending dislike requests to a private server where they are stored and requested, bypassing the video uploader's attempt to hide the count. It is important to note that this feature does not violate YouTube's terms of service or any laws, as it does not modify or interfere with the video or the YouTube platform.
